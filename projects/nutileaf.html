NUTRILEAF

Low-cost portable microscope-level camera for detecting nutrient deficiencies in corn leaves using image processing.

Objective

To develop a non-destructive, affordable system capable of identifying nutrient deficiencies in maize leaves using computer vision and deep learning.

Technologies Used

  • Python
  • OpenCV
  • TensorFlow
  • Custom imaging hardware

Outcome

Achieved accurate detection of visible nutrient stress indicators, enabling early intervention for crop health management.
